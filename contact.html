Contact

How to reach Arcane Labs.

For anything at all, hello@arcanelabs.info is a good first stop. The addresses below route to the right inbox if you'd prefer to be specific.

── CHANNELS ───────────────────────────────────────────────
  • General — hello@arcanelabs.info. Questions, partnerships, press, and anything that doesn't fit below.
  • Product support — support@arcanelabs.info. Help with Longeviti, Vael, or any project in the catalog.
  • Security — security@arcanelabs.info. Vulnerability reports.
  • Privacy — privacy@arcanelabs.info. Data requests, deletion, or privacy policy questions.
  • Open source — github.com/arcanelabsio. Bug reports, feature requests, contributions.
── LOCATION ───────────────────────────────────────────────

Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
