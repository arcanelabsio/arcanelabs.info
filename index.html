Privacy-first tools for personal data sovereignty.

Arcane Labs is an independent software studio. We build tools that keep your data where it belongs — on your device, in your cloud storage, under your control.

Products GitHub
Principles
01

Zero-server by default

Your data lives in your own cloud storage or on your device. We don't operate servers that hold user data. No accounts to create, no databases to breach.

02

Bring your own AI

When a product uses AI, it runs in your AI assistant's session — on your machine, billed to your account. We publish open schemas. Your AI writes files that conform.

03

Open contracts

Data formats, schemas, and skills are open-source. You can inspect, fork, and extend them. Vendor lock-in happens at the data layer — so we make the data layer yours.
