Intelligent tools that run on your own infrastructure.

We build systems that pair structured data on your cloud storage with AI that runs in your own session. No servers in between. Your data stays yours — not as a compromise, but because the architecture is better without a middleman.

Products GitHub
How we build
01

User-owned storage as infrastructure

Google Drive, iCloud, local filesystem — not backup destinations, but the primary data layer. We build structured schemas, sync protocols, and indexed file-based data stores on top of storage you already control.

02

Client-side intelligence

AI runs in your session, on your machine, billed to your account. The app is a renderer and logger. The intelligence layer produces structured output that conforms to open schemas — no API proxy, no round-trip to our servers.

03

Open schema contracts

Every data format is published, versioned, and MIT-licensed. Any AI can produce plans the app renders. Any tool can read the data you've generated. The interoperability layer is the product — not the lock-in.
