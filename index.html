How we build
01

Contracts before code

Schemas, interfaces, and architectural decisions are written first; implementations follow. Every non-trivial choice lives as an ADR in the project's docs/adr/ — future-us gets the why, not just the what.

02

Small surface per product

One thing done well beats five things half-done. Each product is scoped so a small team can ship it and support it. Architecture is chosen for the problem, not imposed by an org-wide playbook.

03

AI-augmented, not AI-dependent

We use AI assistants heavily in our own workflow, and we build tooling (Forge, practitioner workflows) that assumes AI is part of the toolkit. But the products degrade gracefully when the model isn't there.
