Products

Intelligent systems built on storage you already control. Each product pairs structured data layers with client-side compute — no servers between you and your data.

L

Longeviti

Live · Android

Personal longevity tracking that turns blood work and daily habits into an adaptive health plan. Upload lab reports, and your own AI assistant — Claude Code, Codex, or any compatible CLI — generates personalized meal plans, supplement schedules, and weekly coaching.

Under the hood: a structured data layer on Google Drive with bidirectional sync, schema-validated JSON files, reconciliation on load, and debounced auto-sync. The app is a renderer and logger. All reasoning runs in your AI session, on your machine.

Flutter · Google Drive sync · Open JSON schemas · Claude Code skills
