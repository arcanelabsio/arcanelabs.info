V

Vael

In development

A local-first family finance app for Indian households — accounts, expenses, net worth, shared across family members without surrendering data to anyone.

Every byte of financial data is encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM before it leaves your device. Google Drive is used only as blind ciphertext transport: we have no servers, and a Drive scan sees only random noise. Recovery works via a family passphrase, not an email flow — by design.

Flutter · Client-side AES-GCM · Drive-as-blind-transport · Zero-server
