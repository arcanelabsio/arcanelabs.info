This is the first post on arcanelabs.info.

Arcane Labs is an independent software studio. Everything published
here is built around three rules: *your data should stay yours*,
*the best features don't need a server*, and *complexity should
live in the architecture — not in the user's face*.

In the coming weeks we'll start publishing:

- user guides for the libraries in the catalog — how to install
  and use `drive_sync_flutter`, `forge`, and `shellcraft` in your
  own projects
- design notes on the apps in active build — what local-first
  actually looks like when you sit down to ship it
- occasional essays on reusability, on the discipline of doing
  less, and on privacy as architecture rather than policy

If any of that sounds interesting, bookmark
[/writing/](/writing/) or follow the
[GitHub org](https://github.com/arcanelabsio) — both will have
new material as it lands.

More soon.
